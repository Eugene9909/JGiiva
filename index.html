Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?

+

We recommend booking as early as possible — ideally 9–18 months before your wedding date. Popular summer and bank holiday weekends fill up quickly, especially for South African and African weddings which often span multiple days.

Do you travel to South Africa for weddings?

+

Absolutely. We have extensive experience photographing weddings in Cape Town, Johannesburg, Durban and beyond. Travel packages are available — please enquire for destination wedding pricing.

How long until we receive our photos?

+

You'll receive a sneak peek gallery within 48–72 hours of your wedding. The full edited gallery is delivered within 6–8 weeks. Wedding films typically take 8–12 weeks depending on the package.

Do you cover both traditional and white weddings?

+

Yes — this is our speciality. Many of our couples celebrate across two or more days, covering the traditional engagement ceremony, the introduction, and the church or court wedding. We have packages designed for full multi-day coverage.

What is included in the deposit to secure a date?

+

A non-refundable booking fee of 25% of your chosen package secures your date exclusively. The remaining balance is due 4 weeks before the wedding. We accept bank transfer and all major cards.

Can we customise a package?

+

Of course. Our packages are a starting point. We're happy to add extra hours, a second shooter, engagement sessions, same-day edits, or premium albums. Get in touch and we'll build something bespoke for your day.
